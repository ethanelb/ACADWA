West Africa · Lomé, Togo

Creating Business Opportunities in Tennessee & Togo — West African Production Line

ACADWA's factory in Lomé is more than a production site — it is an economic engine for West Africa, creating direct employment and driving a broader ecosystem of indirect opportunity across the region.

Tennessee Distribution Hub

Based in Cleveland, TN, ACADWA drives local opportunity through regional distribution partnerships, last-mile logistics, and retail supply chain integration across the Southeast.

Supply Chain Ripple Effect

Our operations support local suppliers, transporters, packaging partners and distributors. Each direct job at ACADWA generates an estimated 3–4 indirect positions in the broader Togolese economy.

A Strategic Location

Lomé is West Africa's fastest-growing port city. ACADWA leverages its proximity to the Port of Lomé — one of the most modern deepwater ports on the African Atlantic coast — for efficient regional and international distribution.
